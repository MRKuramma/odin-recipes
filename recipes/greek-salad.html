Greek Salad

Description:

This is a traditional Greek Salad with green pepper, tomato, cucumber, red onion and feta cheese.
If you have like kalamata olives, add them for extra color and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 4 Large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1 Cucumber, peeled and chopped
  • 1 Red Onion, chopped
  • 3 Ounces crumbled Feta Cheese
  • ¼ Cup Olive Oil
  • ⅛ Cup Lemon juice
  1. In a large bowl, combine the tomatoes, green bell pepper, cucumber, red onion, olive oil, and lemon juice.
  2. Refrigerate until thoroughly chilled
  3. Sprinkle with feta cheese before serving.
